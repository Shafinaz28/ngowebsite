Our Story

Founded in 2020 (formally registered in 2026) by Bondili Sai Manoj and Prasad Majji, Vishv Kraanti Samaaj Foundation began with one dream: rural children should never be left behind due to lack of resources.

From our beginnings in Andhra Pradesh, we have grown into a movement that guides rural students toward urban education, real-world skills, and financial independence.

Programs That Create Change

We focus on measurable outcomes—education access, skill readiness, and long-term financial stability.

Rural-to-Urban Education Bridge

Scholarships, mentorship and logistical support to help rural students study in urban schools & colleges.

Target: 7–22 yrs • Status: Ongoing

Skill Development Workshops

Computer literacy, communication skills and career readiness workshops for modern opportunities.

Target: 15–25 yrs • Status: Ongoing

Financial Literacy & Stability

Guidance on savings, entrepreneurship and career planning to ensure long-term financial independence.

Target: Rural college students • Status: Ongoing
