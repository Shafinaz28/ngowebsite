About Us

Empowering rural students to access education, opportunity, and financial independence.

A dream that began in rural Andhra Pradesh—now a growing movement.

Vishv Kraanti Samaaj Foundation was founded in 2020 (formally registered in 2026) by Bondili Sai Manoj and Prasad Majji with a simple yet powerful dream: to ensure that children from rural communities are not left behind in the race for education and opportunity.

Born out of firsthand experiences of seeing bright young minds struggle due to lack of resources, our NGO began its journey in Andhra Pradesh. Since then, we have been registered under MCA as a Non-Profit Organisation (Section 8 Company) and have grown into a mission-driven initiative that uplifts rural students—guiding them toward urban education and financial independence.

Mission & Vision

Education is the strongest tool to break the cycle of poverty—and we exist to make it reachable.

Our Mission

We believe that education is the strongest tool to break the cycle of poverty. Rural children often face barriers that prevent them from accessing quality schooling—and this limits their future prospects.

Our mission is clear: To empower rural students by bridging the gap between village classrooms and urban opportunities, helping them become financially stable and socially confident individuals.

Our Vision

We envision a future where every child, regardless of where they are born, has equal access to education and the chance to thrive.

Our long-term goal is to create a society where rural communities are transformed by educated, financially independent youth who contribute positively to the nation’s growth.

What We Stand For

Our values guide every program, partnership, and child we support.

Empathy

We understand realities on the ground and act with care.
