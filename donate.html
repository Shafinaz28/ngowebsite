Donation FAQs

Quick answers for donors.

How will my donation be used?

Based on the purpose you choose—education, skills, financial literacy, or general fund.

Will I get an acknowledgement?

Yes. Enable “Email receipt” and we will send an acknowledgement to your email.

Can I donate monthly?

Yes. Connect the “Proceed to Pay” button to a monthly plan on your payment gateway.

Can my company donate via CSR?

Yes. Use the “Talk to Us” button to request proposals and reports.
